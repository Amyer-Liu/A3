What is Burger Ops

Burger Ops is a discussion-based card game that simulates real fast-food industry challenges during supply chain disruptions. Players act as managers racing to assemble their signature burgers while navigating obstacles, debating solutions, and managing limited resources.

Card Types

The game uses five card types to guide problem-solving, strategic trading, and ingredient collection. The first player to complete their burger earns the "Manager of the Year" title.

My Role

As part of a group project, I designed the core game mechanics, rule system, and card structure. The game encourages critical thinking, teamwork, and adaptive decision-making, reflecting key service design principles in an engaging format.

Why needs redesign

The original Purple Bubble Tea website needed a redesign because its visuals were flat and inconsistent, the content lacked personality and clarity, and the navigation was confusing for users. Important information was difficult to read, interactive elements were unclear, and the overall design failed to reflect the brand's warmth or identity. A redesign was necessary to improve flow, usability, and brand trust.

What changed

The redesigned website introduces a warmer colour palette, improved typography, and clearer visual hierarchy to enhance readability and brand warmth. Layouts were reorganized for clarity, including a structured timetable for store hours and a more visible contact form. High-quality product images and more personable, brand-focused content strengthen storytelling and credibility. Navigation and interactivity were simplified, making key information easier to find and improving overall usability and consistency.
